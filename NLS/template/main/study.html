  2. Study Material
    For Overtaking Vehicles
  • You should overtake from the right side
  • Overtaking should not be done at places where overtaking prohibition signs are posted, busy intersections or lanes, zebra crossings, bends, bends, uphill and downhill, etc.
  • When trying to overtake a vehicle coming from behind, you should slow down and give way.
  • The unbroken straight lines in the middle the road, means you should not overtake by crossing the line. from opposite lane.
  • It is reasonable to overtake if your vehicle is faster than the one in front of you.
  • While overtaking, one should pay attention to the fact that the vehicle is not coming from the opposite direction
  • The broken lines in the middle of a road, means you can overtake from the opposite lane.
